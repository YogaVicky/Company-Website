About us

We’ve been doing leather export agency since 2007

Apex International is moving ahead to serve the clients all over the world. A well established organization really capable of connecting India with the global market, as a leather agency we are always in service of our customers and clients from material research, development of articles, strict quality control and inspecting of leathers in various stages adhering the testing norms on final product and delivery of the goods at the destination on time.
The real strength of Apex International is its ability to seamlessly continue quality and excellent prices. With the vast experience and knowledge for more than 25 yrs in the field in India and global market, we advocate for quality, in time delivery and the best prices for suppliers and buyers.
Apex International represents and helps the tanneries to grow in the domestic as well as in export market adhering to the norms of Leather Working Group (LWG) .




The CEO of the company S.Varadharajan is very vastly experienced as a technical manager in reputed handbag factory in China and reputed chemical factory in Thailand associated with international brands in the leather production,leather finishing and chemical(Italian) fields.He has spent 25 years of his life in new leather article development,leather quality inspection,leather grading as per 'SATRA' and testing norms as per 'REACH' standard in China,Taiwan,HongKong and India.
